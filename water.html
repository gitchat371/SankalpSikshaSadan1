Clean Drinking Water

Our school provides clean and safe drinking water for all students. Water quality is regularly checked and maintained. Proper filtration systems are installed. Hygiene is given top priority in water facilities. Students are encouraged to stay hydrated throughout the day. Clean water points are available in different areas of the school. Regular maintenance ensures continuous supply. Awareness about hygiene is also provided. Safe drinking water helps maintain good health. We ensure that every student has access to pure drinking water.
