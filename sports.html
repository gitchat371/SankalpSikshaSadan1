Sports Ground

Our school has a large and well-maintained sports ground for physical activities. Students are encouraged to participate in various sports. Regular games and competitions are organized. Physical fitness is considered an important part of education. The ground is suitable for multiple sports activities. Students learn teamwork and discipline through sports. Qualified staff guide students during practice. Safety measures are followed during all activities. Sports help in overall development of students. We believe in balancing academics with physical health.
